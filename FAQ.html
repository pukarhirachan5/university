How It Works?

How do I get started with using the platform? ▼

To get started, simply sign up, complete your profile, and schedule your first appointment. You can choose a therapist and find a program that suits your needs.

How does the mental health assessment process work? ▼

The mental health assessment is conducted by your therapist during your first session. It helps to determine the best course of treatment for you.

What type of support can I expect from the platform? ▼

You can expect a range of support, including therapy sessions, educational resources, and progress tracking, all tailored to your needs.

How much does everything cost? ▼

The cost depends on the program you select and your insurance coverage. You can find detailed pricing on the 'Programs' page or contact support for more information.
